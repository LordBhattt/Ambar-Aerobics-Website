Our Story

More Than Two Decades of Fitness

Founded in 2004 by Mr. Ambar Lakhotia, Ambar Aerobics began as a small, single-studio venture with a simple mission: to make fitness enjoyable and accessible for everyone. What started as a passion project has grown into one of Mumbai's most respected and recognized fitness institutes.

Over the past two decades, we've helped thousands of Mumbaikars get fit, stay healthy, and fall in love with working out. Our unique approach blends fun, high-energy group classes with professional-grade fitness education, creating a holistic ecosystem for both fitness enthusiasts and aspiring trainers.

Today, with 16 branches across Mumbai, we continue to expand our reach while staying true to our core belief: Where Fitness is Fun!

For Our Members:

We offer a vibrant range of group fitness classes including Aerobics, Zumba, Power Yoga, Pilates, Kickboxing, and Functional Training. Our expert-led sessions are designed to accommodate all fitness levels — from complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Every class is an experience: great music, infectious energy, and a supportive community that celebrates every milestone with you.

For Our Students:

We are an ISO-certified fitness training institute offering professional diploma and certification courses. Our programs in Aerobics, Yoga, Pilates, and Nutrition provide comprehensive, industry-ready training. With expert instructors, a detailed curriculum covering anatomy and exercise science, and dedicated job placement assistance, we equip our graduates to launch successful careers in the fitness industry.
